HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT

As a Admin,Doctor and Patient you can use below services based on assigned role.

    ADMIN CAN DO
  • 1.SIGNUP THEIR ACCOUNT. THEN LOGIN (NO APPROVAL REQUIRED)
  • 2.APPROVE THOSE DOCTOR WHO APPLIED FOR JOB IN THEIR HOSPITAL
  • 3.DISCHARGE PATIENT WHEN TREATMENT IS DONE
  • 4.GENERATE INVOICE ACCORDING TO MEDICINE COST, ROOM CHARGE, DOCTOR CHARGE AND OTHER CHARGE
  • 5.APPROVE THOSE APPOINTMENTS WHICH IS REQUESTED BY PATIENT
    DOCTOR CAN DO
  • 1.APPLY FOR JOB IN HOSPITAL
  • 2.APPROVAL REQUIRED BY HOSPITAL ADMIN, THEN ONLY DOCTOR CAN LOGIN
  • 3.CAN ONLY VIEW THEIR PATIENT DETAILS (SYMPTOMS, NAME, MOBILE ) ASSIGNED TO THAT DOCTOR BY ADMIN
  • 4.CAN VIEW THEIR DISCHARGED(BY ADMIN)
  • 5.CAN VIEW THEIR APPOINTMENTS, BOOKED BY ADMIN
  • 6.CAN DELETE THEIR APPOINTMENT, WHEN DOCTOR ATTENDED THEIR APPOINTMENT
    PATIENT CAN DO
  • 1.CREATE ACCOUNT FOR ADMIT IN HOSPITAL
  • 2.APPROVAL REQUIRED BY HOSPITAL ADMIN, THEN ONLY PATIENT CAN LOGIN
  • 3.CAN VIEW ASSIGNED DOCTOR'S DETAILS LIKE ( SPECIALIZATION, MOBILE, ADDRESS).
  • 4.CAN VIEW THEIR BOOKED APPOINTMENT STATUS (PENDING/CONFIRMED BY ADMIN).
  • 5.CAN BOOK APPOINTMENTS.(APPROVAL REQUIRED BY ADMIN)
  • 6.CAN VIEW/DOWNLOAD INVOICE PDF (ONLY WHEN THAT PATIENT IS DISCHARGED BY ADMIN).
